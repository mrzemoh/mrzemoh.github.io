ArcoType – Effortless Curved Text for Figma

ArcoType is a Figma plugin that allows you to create curved text with ease. With just a few clicks, you can transform your text into a curved shape, making it look more dynamic and engaging. Whether you're creating a poster, a banner, or a design for a website, ArcoType can help you create a unique and eye-catching design.

Key Features

  • Instant Circular & Arc Text: Easily bend any text along a perfect circle or custom arc with intuitive controls.
  • Live Preview: See exactly how your curved text will look before placing it on the canvas.
  • Font-Perfect Accuracy: ArcoType measures real font metrics—including bold and italic styles—for flawless alignment and spacing.
  • Full Customization: Adjust radius, spacing, vertical offset, arc direction, and rotation to match any creative vision.
  • Smart UI: Modern, responsive interface that fits seamlessly into your Figma workflow.
  • One-Click Placement: Instantly add your curved text to the canvas, perfectly centered and grouped for easy editing.

Why Designers Love ArcoType

  • Saves hours of manual adjustments and tedious path editing.
  • Works with all your fonts—system and Google Fonts included.
  • No more guesswork: what you see in the preview is exactly what you get on the canvas.
